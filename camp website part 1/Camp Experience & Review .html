A camping trip can be an exciting and memorable experience, allowing you to connect with nature and spend time away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Before setting out on a camping trip, you will need to plan and prepare carefully. This can involve researching the location, packing appropriate gear and equipment, and ensuring you have enough food and water to sustain yourself during the trip.

We're also bringing a big top, which will provide shelter throughout the night as well as hosting talks and demonstrations from an impressive range of guest speakers.

Overall, a camping trip can be an enriching and rewarding experience, allowing you to disconnect from the stresses of everyday life and connect with nature in a meaningful way!
